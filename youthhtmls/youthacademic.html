Stress due to Academic/job Issues

Things may be depressing in academia, but you don’t have to be depressed. First and foremost, if you already have symptoms of depression, get diagnosed and treated right away.

    Identify Stress Symptoms

The psychological symptoms of depression include:
  • continuous low mood or sadness
  • feeling hopeless and helpless
  • having low self-esteem
  • feeling tearful
  • feeling guilt-ridden
  • feeling irritable and intolerant of others
  • having no motivation or interest in things
  • finding it difficult to make decisions
  • not getting any enjoyment out of life

    • 1. Incorporate more anticipation into your life.

    • 2. Do regular exercise.
    • 3. Surround yourself with supportive people.
    • 4. Stop comparing yourself with others.
    • 5. Learn to be assertive and stand up for yourself.
    • 6. Understand your working environment and your advisor’s expectations.
    • 7. Avoid isolation.
